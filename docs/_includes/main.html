The ELIXIR Single-Cell Omics (SCO) Community brings together people from all over Europe to collaborate on training, benchmarking, and data standards in single-cell and spatial omics, building on top of national experiences and paving the way towards integrated long-term solutions.

JOIN US

If you are working on single-cell or spatial omics and would like to participate, please contact us!

TRAINING

We provide training, collect training materials and capacitate new trainers.

TOOLS

We collect benchmarking datasets and organize SpaceHacks and workshops.

DATA

We collaborate on defining standards for single-cell and spatial data and metadata.
